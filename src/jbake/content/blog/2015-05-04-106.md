<div>Take a list of languages:</div>
<div></div>
<div>English</div>
<div>French</div>
<div>Spanish</div>
<div>Portugues</div>
<div></div>
<div>And you want a regex that matches everything except English</div>
<div></div>
<div>using negative lookahead</div>
<div></div>
<div>(?im)^(?!English).+$</div>
<div></div>
<div>using only (?!English).+ will not work.</div>
